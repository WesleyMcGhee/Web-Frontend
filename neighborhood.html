Blooming Prairie

Location

Located in Southeast Minnesota, Blooming Prairie Boast a population of 1991, with a couple of Restruants, and Cafes it is a quaint town. There is a big farming communtity, and you can tell if you would walk into our local Gas Station in the morning, you can see all the retired farms there drinking coffee, and eating breakfast. Although a small town it has the biggest parade in Minnesoata.

Things To Do

Although there is not a lot to do in Blooming Prairie, We have two parks, and a swimming pool. That is where you will find most kids in the summer, there is also a big long board community here. In the winter people will put up there swim suits, and long boards, and they will go Snow Mobiling, that is one of the more common methods for kids that live in the country to get to school. The final thing is one day in the spring the school will have a tractor day, where everyone brings there tractor to school, and they have a parade through the town.
